The Cry For Help

Canada is home to about 630 species of birds but about 15 per cent are at risk of extinction. Predators, invasive species, climate change, disease, and toxic chemicals are among many dangers birds face in their short lives.


The nest egg:

Like you, birds need a spot to call home to survive. They need the four basics: food, water, shelter, and space.
